PRIVACY POLICY ============== UTC Timezone Converter Extension Version 3.2 Last Updated: March 2026 DATA COLLECTION --------------- This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user information. LOCAL STORAGE ONLY ------------------ This extension uses browser local storage exclusively for: - Saving your preferred timezone selection - Saving your panel visibility preference These preferences are stored only on your device and are NEVER: - Transmitted to any server - Shared with third parties - Used for any analytics or tracking PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED --------------------- The extension requires the following permissions for core functionality: • Host Permissions (): Required to detect and convert times displayed on any webpage you visit. This allows the extension to read time text on pages you view. • Storage: Used only to save your personal preferences (selected timezone, panel visibility) locally in your browser. • Clipboard Write: Allows you to copy converted UTC times to your clipboard when you choose to do so. • Active Tab: Used to communicate with the current webpage to show or hide the time panel. NO TRACKING ----------- We do not use: - Analytics or tracking scripts - Cookies - Web beacons - Third-party services - Remote servers CHILDREN'S PRIVACY ------------------ This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
