Privacy Policy

UTC Timezone Converter Extension | Version 3.2
Last Updated: March 2026
This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user information.

Local Storage Usage

This extension uses browser local storage exclusively to improve your experience. Your preferences are stored only on your device and are never:

  • Transmitted to any server
  • Shared with third parties
  • Used for any analytics or tracking

We use this storage specifically for saving your preferred timezone selection and your panel visibility preference.

Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions to function correctly:

Host Permissions: Required to detect and convert times displayed on the webpages you visit.
Storage: Used to save your personal preferences locally in your browser.
Clipboard Write: Allows you to copy converted UTC times to your clipboard.
Active Tab: Used to communicate with the current webpage to show or hide the time panel.

No Tracking

We value your privacy. We do not use analytics, cookies, web beacons, or any third-party remote servers to monitor your activity.
